看《纸牌屋》学英语 S1

Author: Ng Wai Chun Date: Monday, 10 June, 2024  23:00:38

The nature of promises, is that they remain immune to changing circumstances.

所谓的承诺就是不论情况如何变化也不受影响。

If it had been up to me, I wouldn’t have waited this long to tell you.

如果由我决定，我也不会现在才告诉你。

You certainly have my undivided attention .

我洗耳恭听。

Not a single sentence, word, comma, or period.

一句话，一个词，一个逗号，一个句点都不是。

If memory serves me, it was Mr. Kapeniak who wrote it.

如果我没记错，作者正是科佩尼科先生写的。

Let me further say that I’m a strong support of it.

让我进一步阐述，我是它强烈的支持者。

He chose money over power. In this town, a mistake nearly everyone makes.

他选择金钱而非权力，这个城市，几乎人人都犯了这个错。

All we have here really is a perception problem.

其实就看你是站在什么角度了。

We don’t get a second chance at a first impression.

第一印象只有一次。

I’m on your side.

我站在你这边。

They want to point fingers.

他们要找人怪罪。

I’m up to here with them, for lying, for turning their back on you.

我受够他们了，谎话连篇。落井下石。

What a martyr craves more than anything is a sword to fall on,

壮士最渴望的就是壮烈牺牲。

I’m not a wheeler dealer.

我不会精明算计

Let me be on the sidelines for this.

让我置身事外吧。

Well, I could only consider that as an option if I knew that I could still come to you for counsel.

要我考虑接受，你得保证给我提建议。

Truth be told I never really knew him or what his dreams were.

其实，我并不了解他也不知道他的梦想

After all, what is faith, if it doesn’t endure when we are tested the most.

信仰是什么，不就是在考研最严峻时依旧能坚持到底吗？

What else can we do but take what seems meaningless and try to make something meaningful from it.

除了从无意义的事中尽力挖掘出一些有意义的东西，我们还能做什么呢。

Do you think that’s just luck and a firm handshake?

你认为光凭运气和握手就能做到吗？

What could possibly be more pressing than me?

还有什么事能比我还急？

Nothing will come of this.

这样做毫无用处。

You ought to take better care of yourself too.

你也应该照顾好自己

There’s forces bigger than either of us at play here.

有些势力是你我无力抗衡的

I will make it up to you.

在未来我会补偿你。

Most politicians are permanently chained to that slogan.

大多数政客终生都被这句口号所束缚。

I will make that hypocrisy hurt.

你就得为这份虚伪付出代价。

There is the sort of thing that there’s no turning back.

这种事一旦决定就没有回头路了

It’s so refreshing to work with someone who will throw a saddle on a gift horse rather than look it in the mouth.

和这样的人合作真令人振奋，对到手的礼物不吹毛求疵，而能让它物尽其用。

People in this town don’t go out of their way to be nice to each other

这座城市里的人，不会费心善待他人

She’s getting the better side of the bargain.

她收益比较大

The education reform and achievement act that I sign into law today will affect every child and parent in the United States， will ensure a better educated workforce for decades to come.

由我签署并于今天生效的《教育改革和成就法案》，与全国的孩子及家长息息相关，它将改善未来数十年的教学水平。

This is not a hail Mary.

这不是听天由命。

I realize that I sometimes think out of the box.

我知道我有时会打破常规。

We won’t stand in your way.

我们不会反对你。

There’s no better way to overpower a trickle of double than with a flood of naked truth.
打破疑虑最好的利器莫过于赤裸裸的事实。

Russo’s planted his feet on the wrong side of the fence.

罗素这是站错队了。

There will come a time to put Remy in my crosshairs.

总有一天我的准星会对向雷米。

Proximity to power deludes some into believing they wield it.

接近权力让一些人错以为他们拥有权力

I put an end to that sort of thinking before it begins.

我要把这种错觉扼杀在萌芽里

Sh was never more than a faint blip on my radar.

她于我微若尘埃。

You don’t have to mince words.

没必要拐弯抹角。

Don’t step out of the sunlight for no reason

别无缘无故没事找事

Not one who bites off more than he can chew.

而不是个不自量力的人

Then have faith you made the right choice

那就相信您做出了正确的决定

This is about the big picture

我们得为大局着想

There is no sacred ground for the conquered.

成者王，败者寇

I have to distance myself from you.

我得远离你。

And given where we are, Frank, I’m not inclined to follow your suggestions

基于目前的情况，我不是很想接受你的建议

I wasn’t suggesting it as something you should do.

我不是说您该这么做

I think it’s better to drag things out as long as possible.

我觉得这种事拖得越久越好

The heart can choke the mind when all its blood flows back unto itself

感情用事，理智就会收到影响

I can never fill your shoes.

我永远无法与您比肩

原创 Ng Wai Chun
